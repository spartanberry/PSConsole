Pode is NOT bundled in this artifact (it must be vendored once from an
internet-connected STAGING box, since the target PSCONSOLE01 is offline).

On a staging machine WITH internet, run:

    Save-Module -Name Pode -RequiredVersion 2.* -Path .\_stage
    Copy the resulting Pode\<version>\* into THIS folder (modules\Pode\),
    so that modules\Pode\Pode.psd1 exists at this path.

Then re-zip the bundle and transfer to PSCONSOLE01.
The installer asserts modules\Pode\Pode.psd1 exists and will abort if missing.
